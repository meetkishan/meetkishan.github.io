This site is a new home for my digital footprint — my writing, my travel photos, and my work.

## Why rebuild?

My old portfolio did its job, but it was a single static HTML page. I wanted a place where I can simply write a markdown file and publish — no database, no server, no CMS lock-in.

The whole site is generated from `.md` files with [Nuxt Content](https://content.nuxt.com), styled with Tailwind, and deployed as plain static HTML. The source is version-controlled, portable and mine.

## What lives here

- **Blog** — thoughts, experiences and my journey, written in markdown.
- **Gallery** — travel albums with photos and stories.
- **Portfolio** — my work, experience and the SaaS products I've built.
